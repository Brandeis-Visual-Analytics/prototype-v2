How to test:

You may need to go to the Privacy & Security setting to turn on Developer Mode
1. Open Xcode and create a new project HapticBarPOC
    a. You may have to click on the project and scroll down to “Frameworks, Libraries…” and add “CoreHaptics.framework” 
2. Connect your iPhone to your Mac (you may need to use a USB-C cable to do that) and press run 
You may need to trust the developer on your phone (Settings → General → VPN & Device Management)
